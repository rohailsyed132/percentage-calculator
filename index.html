what is a percentage?

a percentage is basically a number written as a fraction of 100.

x percent of y formula=(x*y)/100

formula for what is x percent of y=(x*y)/100 for example 5 percent of 100 is 5 so we multiply 5 times 100 divided by 100 and we get 5

x is what percent of y formula=(x/y)*100

formula for x is what percent of y=(x/y)*100 for example 50 percent of 100 is 50 so we divide 50 by 100 and then times 100 and we get 50

percentage increase/decrease formula=(new value-old value)/old value*100

formula for percentage increase/decrease= (new value-old value)/old value times 100 for example 5 to 6 is 20 percent increase so we do 6-5 which is 1 then divide by 5 and then we times 100 and we get 20
